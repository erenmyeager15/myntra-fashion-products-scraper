# Myntra Fashion Products Scraper Promotion Notes

## Positioning

Collect structured public Myntra fashion product listings for catalog research, price tracking, discount monitoring, and assortment analysis.

## Best small demo

- Search query: `tshirts`
- Category paths: empty
- Maximum results: `1`
- Sort: `recommended`
- Proxy: Residential India

Show the input, one clean product row, image preview, price/MRP/discount fields, and JSON or CSV export.

## LinkedIn draft

I published a Myntra Fashion Products Scraper on Apify for structured public fashion catalog and price research.

It collects public product fields such as title, brand, price, MRP, discount percentage, rating, rating count, available sizes, category, product URL, and image URL.

The safest first run is intentionally tiny: tshirts, one result, recommended sort, and Residential India proxy. Inspect the output, then expand to more queries, category paths, result limits, or sort orders.

Useful for fashion price tracking, brand assortment research, discount monitoring, and marketplace dashboards.

## Reddit or Discord draft

I built an Apify Actor that turns public Myntra search/category pages into structured JSON/CSV product data. It supports search queries, category paths or URLs, result limits, sort order, and Residential India proxy settings.

For a low-cost check, run tshirts + one result with Residential India proxy. Feedback on the schema and useful fashion-commerce fields is welcome.
